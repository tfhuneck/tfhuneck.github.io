About


My name is Timothy Frances Jeremy Huneck, and I am a recent graduate from the University of California Davis
with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Political Science and a focus in International Relations and Economics. My current objective is to pursue a career in Product / Program Management in the Tech Industry. The combination of my education, skills, personal interests, and experience makes me a very suitable candidate for this role.

The experience of over 10 years in customer service and 2 years in product and e-commerce management have earned me an extensive and vital skillset. I started my career with an apprenticeship in the hotel industry, learning the ins and outs of every department and earning an official hotel industry expert certification from the Hotel industry school in Hamburg, Germany. I learned to multitask and work under pressure with attention to detail to deliver a luxury customer experience. Above anything else, I developed outstanding communication skills. After my apprenticeship, I moved to the United States to attend college and managed to receive various promotions during my employment in the service industry. For the past two years, I took on the opportunity to be an operational and events manager for the Open Rice Restaurant Group Inc. with the objective to grow the company. Along with this position I also assumed the tasks of product and E-Commerce management as well as overseeing the marketing of these companies. I am now looking to apply my versatile skill set in the tech industry.

My affinity for technology and design paired with an entrepreneurial mindset and the ambition to develop invigorating products have long drawn me to the tech industry. I have started and participated in various projects that showcase my passion and understanding of software products. These projects include the development of a startup restaurant inventory app, a price web scraper that effectively enhanced market research and competitive pricing, as well as joining a game development group as a product manager.
